# About the Framework
This framework is developed by the [Institute for Artificial Intelligence](https://ai.uni-bremen.de/) and [DFG-funded CRC 1320 EASE](https://ease-crc.org). The institute is headed by [Prof. Michael Beetz](https://ai.uni-bremen.de/team/michael_beetz).
